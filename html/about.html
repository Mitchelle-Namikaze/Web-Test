Meet our CEO

Swipe to see more photos

N'yillah Borteh Conteh is a dedicated student, entrepreneur, and visionary. She is the founder of Borteh_oo1, a thriving brand that specializes in perfumes and jewelry, offering products that embody elegance, confidence, and timeless beauty.

Alongside her business success, she is pursuing a degree in Information Technology at Limkokwing University. Her academic path equips her with innovative skills in technology, while her entrepreneurial experience strengthens her leadership, creativity, and customer engagement.

Our Business Story

Her entrepreneurial journey began with just Le 30, a small start that grew through determination and consistency. Today, she sells in wholesale and supplies other vendors who want to start or grow their own businesses. Her story is a true reminder that consistency is key and that great things can come from humble beginnings.

N'yillah is passionate about growth and resilience. She envisions expanding Borteh_oo1 into a recognized brand while inspiring young people especially women to balance education with entrepreneurship and pursue their dreams fearlessly.
